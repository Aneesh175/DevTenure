Common questions
How is the score calculated?
We pull live job postings for your role daily and cross-reference them against your skills. The score reflects skill match rate, market demand trend, and salary positioning — all shown transparently on your results page. No black box.
Why do you need my salary?
Companies replace expensive people first when AI can do the same job cheaper. Your salary tells us if you're economically at risk — not just skill-wise. It's optional, but it makes your score significantly more accurate.
What's actually in the paid roadmap?
Not generic YouTube links. A prioritised week-by-week plan for your specific gaps. Updates every month as the market shifts. If you don't find it useful in 7 days — full refund, no questions asked.
Who built this?
A small independent team. We built this because we couldn't find an honest, data-backed answer to "am I going to be okay?" Everything else was generic advice or expensive coaches. Email us anytime: hello@devtenure.io
What happens to my data?
Never sold individually. We use anonymised patterns to improve the product. Delete everything permanently from account settings anytime. We don't share individual profiles with employers, companies, or recruiters — ever.
Can I cancel anytime?
One click in account settings. No dark patterns, no guilt screens, no hidden fees. Your free score and full breakdown stay accessible even after cancelling Pro.
